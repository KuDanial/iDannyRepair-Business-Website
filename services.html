Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about your repair? Here are answers to the most common questions we receive.

How long will my repair take?

+

Most common repairs (like iPhone screens, iPad batteries, and USB charging docks) are completed within 1 to 2 hours. More complex repairs involving water damage remediation or motherboard microsoldering typically require 24 to 48 hours for diagnostic cycles.

Do you use original OEM parts?

+

We source only original spec parts that match manufacturer standards (OEM grade). In cases where manufacturers do not distribute parts directly to independent workshops, we buy the absolute highest grade components matching original performance specifications, ensuring full compatibility.

What is your "No Fix, No Fee" policy?

+

If we diagnose your device and find that it is irreparable due to fatal mainboard cracks or severe water corrosion, we will not charge you a single cent. You only pay when we successfully return a working device back to you.

Do I need to back up my data before repair?

+

While screen and battery replacements rarely impact device operating storage, we strongly recommend executing a full cloud or computer backup of your personal files before any hardware service to prevent accidental loss.
